1:1 Coaching

Career Coaching
& Mentorship

Coaching is tailored to your specific goals and challenges. Sessions are practical and focused on actionable ways to advance your career.

  • Communication: writing and speaking
  • Product sensibility: user psychology centered approach to thinking about features
  • Product execution: how to successfully land the plane over and over
  • Product strategy: looking at product development as a vital organ of the overall business
  • Technical & systems thinking: demystification and accessible explanation of how things work
  • Reputation & image: help with blindspots and seeing yourself as others might
  • Mock interviews: hiring manager screens, case study presentations, and think-on-your-feet exercises
  • Asking for a raise or promotion: how to successfully plan and achieve your next career goal
