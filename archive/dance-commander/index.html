CodePath Final Project · 2016

Dance Commander

Completed as part of CodePath's 2 month course in iOS development. My personal goal was to gain a deeper understanding of app development and to become a better PM for mobile developers.

My prior programming experience was limited to HTML & CSS, but I learned to build a decent prototype with Xcode and Swift.

Dance Commander is a group project by myself and two other students. I built the functionality of the first view controller — allowing the user to customize a character, the custom transition and image sequence to the second view controller (i.e. that spinning disco ball that is everything), and passing the images from the first to the second view controller.
